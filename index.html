the quotient code

The Quotient Code: Life Forces That Shape Who We Become by Ramesh ‘Mov’ Narayanan is a practical self-improvement and personal development book that helps readers understand how emotions, habits, and mindset influence success, relationships, and everyday decisions.

Using a unique A-to-Z framework of positive and negative “quotients,” this book explains the qualities that help people grow—such as appreciation, empathy, trust, and resilience—and the behaviors that hold them back, including arrogance, toxicity, ego, and pessimism. Each quotient is easy to understand and highly relatable, making this an ideal guide for readers seeking emotional intelligence, self-awareness, and mental balance.

With real-life examples from work and personal life, The Quotient Code helps readers recognize their own patterns of behavior and develop better habits for leadership, career growth, and personal success. The book also includes curated reading recommendations for those who want to explore psychology, motivation, and self-growth more deeply.

By introducing both positive and negative quotients for every letter of the alphabet, the author opens up a whole new lens to understand personality, mindset, and capability. Why ramesh narayanan Writes

Ramesh Narayanan (or 'Mov' as he likes to be called) is a seasoned professional with extensive experience in the Indian IT industry, where he has built a rich and diverse career spanning technology, leadership, and people development. Alongside his corporate journey, he successfully set up and ran a bootstrapped e-commerce startup, gaining first-hand experience in entrepreneurship and business building. He lives in Chennai with his wife and two children, and balances his professional life with a deep passion for travel and self-discovery. A lifelong traveler—physically, mentally, and spiritually—Ramesh sees every journey as a source of insight, learning, and perspective. Driven by a mission to share the lessons from his experiences, he has authored The Quotient Code, focused on telling meaningful stories drawn from work, life, and the road. Through his writing, he hopes to help readers reflect, grow, and make better choices, one quotient at a time.
